About

Pitt Yak is an application built by four students (Christian Boni, Joesph McClain, Philip Washy, & Matthew O'Brien) at the University of Pittsburgh for the Programming for Web Applications course taught by Timothy James. The idea behind this social media project is to allow students at the University of Pittsburgh's main campus to anonymously post their thoughts. Additionally, we allow these students posts to be categorized by 1 of 4 locations. The posts can be filtered by North, Central, and South Oakland as well as Globally for users who are not on campus at the moment.
